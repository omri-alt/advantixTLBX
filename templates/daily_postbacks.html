Kelkoo — daily conversion postbacks

Sends Keitaro GET postbacks from Kelkoo raw reports (per country), Adexa StatsRaw, and Yadore conversion/detail for one stats day (YYYY-MM-DD). Uses the same resume file as the CLI (DAILY_CONVERSION_POSTBACK_STATE_PATH). Dry run does not call postback URLs and does not write resume state. Full Kelkoo (all geos) can take many minutes — ensure your reverse proxy / app worker timeout is high enough.
